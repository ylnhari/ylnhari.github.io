About

I'm a production AI/ML systems engineer with 10+ years of experience shipping models from notebook to real traffic. I specialize in the hardest part of AI: making it actually work at scale in production.

Currently at Best Buy India as Lead AI Engineer, I architect and build the full LLM lifecycle — fine-tuning open-source models with LoRA (Unsloth + PEFT + HuggingFace Accelerate), serving them via vLLM on GKE with HPA autoscaling and Istio routing, and powering retail applications — fraud detection, search, personalization, cybersecurity — at 40,000+ transactions per second. This work replaced external LLM APIs entirely, saving $300K annually while strengthening data governance and compliance.

Before that, I built GPU-accelerated cybersecurity ML at Wipro on NVIDIA DGX (8×A100, 1TB RAM) using NVIDIA Morpheus: behavioral anomaly detection for AWS account security, phishing email classification, and automated abuse inbox routing — double the case volume of the prior pipeline, each threat automatically routed to the right security team.

At Accenture, I led last-mile delivery optimization using Google OR-Tools (Vehicle Routing Problem), scaling a logistics fleet from 130 to 160+ trucks per day, alongside Azure ML quality prediction pipelines for BamaGruppen (Norway) and computer vision defect detection for Amkor (South Korea).

My roots go deeper: time-series forecasting across 8 industrial verticals at GD Research, computer vision at TCS, and ML-powered BI at Infosys — including a stint in Mainframe (COBOL/JCL) that gave me a real appreciation for what "production" means.

The specialist for the gap between "works in a notebook" and "serves real traffic."
